# Career Analytics

Career Analytics are local summaries calculated from ResumeForge data.

## Included Analytics

- Fresh jobs by source
- Average job match score over time
- Applications by status
- Interview conversion rate
- Offer conversion rate
- Salary listing ranges when salary exists on discovered jobs

## Salary Methodology

Salary insights use only salary data already present in discovered or manually imported job listings.

Shown values:

- minimum
- maximum
- midpoint
- average listing midpoint
- highest listed salary
- listing count
- comparison with saved minimum salary preference

ResumeForge labels this as listing-based insight, not market-wide salary research. It does not convert hourly to annual unless a future version explicitly shows the calculation and assumptions.

## Empty States

When there is insufficient data, charts show a plain-language empty state and a useful next action.

## Limitations

- No paid analytics service.
- No internet dependency.
- No fabricated historical data.
- No market salary claim beyond local listing data.
